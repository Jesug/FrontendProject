Indulge in a Symphony of Flavors

Welcome to our culinary paradise, where every dish tells a story of exquisite flavors and tantalizing aromas. Our menu is a meticulously crafted journey through diverse culinary traditions, offering a symphony of taste sensations that will leave you craving for more

Our Menu

Breakfast

Classic American Breakfast

- Scrambled eggs - Bacon or sausage - Toast or English muffin - Hash browns or breakfast potatoes - Greek yogurt or flavored yogurts with granola or honey

Pancakes and Waffles

- Fluffy pancakes with syrup and butter - Belgian waffles with toppings like fresh fruits, whipped cream, and maple syrup

Omelettes

- Cheese omelette with various fillings like mushrooms, onions, peppers, or spinach - Veggie omelette with a mix of fresh vegetables - Ham and cheese omelette

Lunch

Sandwiches and Pizza

- Turkey or chicken club sandwich - BLT (Bacon, Lettuce, Tomato) sandwich - French fries or sweet potato fries - Margherita pizza with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil - Pepperoni pizza

Pasta

- Spaghetti Bolognese (meat sauce) - Fettuccine Alfredo with creamy Parmesan sauce - Pesto pasta with cherry tomatoes and fresh basil

Burgers and Sliders

- Classic beef burger with lettuce, tomato, onion, and choice of toppings - Veggie burger with a selection of plant-based patties - Mini sliders with various fillings like beef, chicken, or pulled pork

Dinner

Appetizers

- Bruschetta with fresh tomatoes, basil, and garlic on toasted bread - Stuffed mushrooms with a savory filling - Spinach and artichoke dip with tortilla chips

Soups and Salads

- Roasted tomato soup with basil - Caesar salad with grilled chicken or shrimp - Caprese salad with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil

Meat Entrées

- Grilled steak with a choice of sauce (pepper, mushroom, or béarnaise) - Herb-roasted chicken with roasted potatoes and seasonal vegetables - Braised lamb shanks with red wine reduction

Dessert

Cookies and Brownies

- Chocolate chip cookies - Oatmeal raisin cookies - Fudgy brownies with walnuts - White chocolate macadamia nut cookies

Ice Cream Treats

- Milkshakes (vanilla, chocolate, or strawberry) - Ice cream floats (root beer float, Coke float) - Ice cream sandwiches with homemade cookies - Banana split with multiple scoops of ice cream, toppings, and whipped cream

Cupcakes and Muffins

- Red velvet cupcakes with cream cheese frosting - Blueberry muffins with a streusel topping - Chocolate cupcakes with buttercream frosting - Carrot cake muffins with cream cheese filling

Cocktail

Mojito

- A refreshing mix of rum, fresh mint leaves, lime juice, sugar, and soda water

Margarita

- A classic cocktail made with tequila, lime juice, and orange liqueur, served on the rocks or frozen with a salt rim

Martini

- A timeless cocktail made with gin or vodka and a hint of vermouth, garnished with an olive or lemon twist

Cosmopolitan

- A popular vodka-based cocktail with cranberry juice, orange liqueur, and a splash of lime juice

Wine

Red Wines

Cabernet Sauvignon: A bold and full-bodied red wine with rich dark fruit flavors, such as blackberry and cassis Merlot: A smooth and medium-bodied red wine with flavors of plum, cherry, and chocolate Pinot Noir: A light and elegant red wine with notes of red berries, cherry, and earthy undertones

Rosé Wines

Provence Rosé: A dry and light-bodied rosé with delicate floral and red fruit flavors White Zinfandel: A sweet and fruity rosé with flavors of strawberry, watermelon, and citrus Sangiovese Rosé: A crisp and refreshing rosé with bright red fruit flavors and a hint of acidity
